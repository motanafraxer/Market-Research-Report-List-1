<p><strong>The Malt Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Diastatic</li><li>Diastatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The malt powder market can be segmented into two primary types: non-diastatic and diastatic malt powders. Non-diastatic malt powder is used primarily as a flavoring and coloring agent in food products, as it does not contain active enzymes for starch conversion. In contrast, diastatic malt powder contains enzymes that break down starches into sugars, making it valuable in baking for promoting fermentation and enhancing dough texture. Both types serve distinct purposes in various culinary and industrial applications.</p></p>
