<p><p>The sensor toilet market features two primary types of sensing technologies: infrared induction and radar sensing. Infrared induction sensors detect motion through heat emitted by the human body, allowing for hands-free operation, enhancing hygiene and convenience. Radar sensing, on the other hand, utilizes microwave signals to detect presence and movement, offering a wider range of detection and increased accuracy. Both technologies aim to improve user experience while promoting cleanliness and efficiency in modern restroom facilities.</p></p>
