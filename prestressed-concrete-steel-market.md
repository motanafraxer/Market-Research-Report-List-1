Bare PC strand features uncoated steel that offers high tensile strength for direct applications. Grease-filled PC strand incorporates a protective grease to minimize corrosion and friction, enhancing durability. Wax-filled PC strand uses wax-based protection, providing moisture resistance and extending lifespan. Additional categories may include additional treatments or hybrid strands that combine features for specialized applications in construction, infrastructure, and precast concrete elements.</p></p>
