<p><p>The adhesives pump market encompasses various types, including diaphragm pumps, lobe pumps, and others. Diaphragm pumps utilize a flexible membrane to create a vacuum for precise adhesive dispensing, making them suitable for diverse applications. Lobe pumps, characterized by their rotating lobes, offer a gentle and consistent flow, ideal for shear-sensitive adhesives. Other pump types may include gear and piston pumps, which cater to specific viscosity and application needs, ensuring efficient and reliable adhesive transfer in various industrial settings.</p></p>
