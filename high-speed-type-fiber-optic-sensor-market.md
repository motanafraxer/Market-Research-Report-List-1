<p><p>The high-speed type fiber optic sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single-mode fiber is designed for long-distance transmission, allowing one light mode to travel, which minimizes signal loss and increases bandwidth. In contrast, multimode fiber supports multiple light modes, making it suitable for shorter distances and higher data rates. Both types are critical for applications like telecommunications, industrial monitoring, and medical diagnostics, with each serving specific performance and distance requirements.</p></p>
