<p><p>The industrial processing pectinase market is primarily divided into two types: acid pectinase and alkaline pectinase. Acid pectinase operates effectively in acidic conditions, making it suitable for fruit juice clarification and wine production. It enhances the extraction of fruit juices by breaking down pectin. In contrast, alkaline pectinase functions optimally in alkaline environments, often used in textile and paper industries for deinking and fiber processing. Both types facilitate the breakdown of pectin, improving product quality and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
