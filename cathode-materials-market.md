<p><p>The cathode materials market encompasses various components essential for battery performance. Active materials are the primary substances that undergo electrochemical reactions, significantly influencing energy density and capacity. Binders, typically polymers, ensure the cohesion of active materials and maintain structural integrity during cycling. Foils, often made of aluminum, serve as conductive substrates that facilitate electron flow and contribute to overall conductivity. Together, these elements play a vital role in enhancing the efficiency, lifespan, and performance of rechargeable batteries.</p></p>
