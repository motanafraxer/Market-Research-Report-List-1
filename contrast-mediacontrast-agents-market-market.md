<p><p>The contrast media market includes various agents used to enhance imaging in medical procedures. Iodinated contrast agents are commonly used in X-rays and CT scans, providing clear vascular imaging. Gadolinium agents are primarily utilized in MRI scans, offering excellent tissue contrast. Barium sulfate is specifically effective for gastrointestinal imaging, allowing detailed visualization of the digestive tract. Microbubble agents enhance ultrasound imaging by improving the contrast of vascular structures, enabling better detection of anomalies in blood flow and tissue characterization.</p></p>
