<p><p>The Double-sided Flexible Printed Circuit (FPC) market comprises two primary types: aluminum-based and copper-based circuits. Aluminum-based FPCs are known for their lightweight, excellent thermal conductivity, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications, including LED lighting and automotive electronics. Conversely, copper-based FPCs offer superior electrical conductivity and reliability, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as smartphones, tablets, and medical devices. Both types cater to diverse industries, driving innovation and efficiency in electronic circuit design.</p></p>
