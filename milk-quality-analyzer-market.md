<p><p>The Milk Quality Analyzer market is segmented into Ultrasonic and Infrared types, each employing distinct technology to assess milk quality. Ultrasonic analyzers use sound waves to measure parameters like fat, protein, and lactose content, providing rapid, precise readings. In contrast, Infrared analyzers use light absorption at specific wavelengths to determine compositional elements, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. Both types are essential for dairy producers, ensuring milk safety, optimizing processing, and meeting quality standards in the industry.</p></p>
