<p><p>The glass printing machine market includes various methods such as screen printing, spray coating, digital printing, and others. Screen printing involves applying ink through a mesh stencil, suitable for large batches. Spray coating uses aerosol technology for even coverage, ideal for intricate designs. Digital printing allows direct printing from a digital file, providing high customization and quality for smaller runs. Other methods may include UV printing or pad printing, catering to diverse needs in decorative and functional glass applications.</p></p>
