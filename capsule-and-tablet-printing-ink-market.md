In contrast, water-based inks are more environmentally friendly, incorporating water as a primary solvent, resulting in lower emissions and safer handling. Both types cater to pharmaceutical needs, with solvent-based inks often preferred for durability, while water-based inks are favored for their sustainability and compliance with health regulations.</p></p>
