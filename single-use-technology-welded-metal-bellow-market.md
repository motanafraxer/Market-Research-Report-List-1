<p><p>The Single Use Technology Welded Metal Bellow market is categorized into two main types: Edge Welded Bellows and Formed Welded Bellows. Edge Welded Bellows are constructed by welding two flat metal sheets along their edges, creating a flexible and adaptable design suitable for various applications in sterile environments. In contrast, Formed Welded Bellows are shaped and welded from a single piece of material, offering robust performance and improved reliability. Both types are essential for maintaining sterile conditions in biotech and pharmaceutical processes.</p></p>
