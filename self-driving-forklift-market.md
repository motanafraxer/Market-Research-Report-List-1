Lidar Navigation relies on laser-based sensors to create detailed 3D maps of the environment, allowing for precise object detection and obstacle avoidance. "Others" may include technologies such as ultrasonic sensors or magnetic navigation systems, catering to diverse operational needs and enhancing automated material handling efficiency.</p></p>
