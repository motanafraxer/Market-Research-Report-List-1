<p><strong>The Radio Control Servo Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cored Motor</li><li>Coreless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Control Servo market is categorized into two main types: cored motors and coreless motors. Cored motors utilize a permanent magnet rotor mounted on a metal core, providing reliable performance and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in budget-friendly applications. In contrast, coreless motors have a rotor that is lighter and more efficient due to the absence of a metal core, offering faster response times and smoother operation, making them preferable for high-performance and precision-oriented tasks.</p></p>
