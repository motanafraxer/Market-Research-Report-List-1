<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CRM software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise CRM is installed locally on a company's servers and requires significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. This option offers complete control over data but demands higher upfront investment. In contrast, cloud-based CRM is hosted on remote servers, accessible via the internet, and usually operates on a subscription model. It offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of use, often with lower initial costs and automatic updates.</p></p>
