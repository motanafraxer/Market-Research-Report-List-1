<p><strong>The Rutile TiO2 Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rutile TiO2 market is primarily divided into two production methods: the sulfate process and the chloride process. The sulfate process involves treating titanium ores with sulfuric acid, yielding lower-quality TiO2, often used in applications like paints and coatings. In contrast, the chloride process utilizes chlorine gas to extract titanium, resulting in high-purity TiO2 suitable for premium applications, including plastics and advanced coatings. Each method significantly impacts production costs, quality, and environmental considerations in the TiO2 market.</p></p>
