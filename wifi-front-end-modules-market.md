<p><p>The WiFi front-end module market encompasses several key components. Power Amplifiers (PAs) enhance signal strength for transmission, critical for maintaining connection quality. Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs) boost weak incoming signals with minimal interference, ensuring effective reception. Switches control signal flow between paths, enabling seamless transitions between transmit and receive modes. Other components may include filters and couplers, which manage noise and optimize performance. Together, these elements are essential for robust and efficient WiFi communication systems.</p></p>
