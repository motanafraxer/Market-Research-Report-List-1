<p><ul><li>Laser Fluorescent Caries Detector</li><li>Fiber Optic Trans-illumination Caries Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental caries detectors market primarily features two types: laser fluorescent caries detectors and fiber optic trans-illumination caries detectors. Laser fluorescent caries detectors utilize laser technology to identify decay by measuring fluorescence emitted from tooth surfaces, allowing for early detection. In contrast, fiber optic trans-illumination caries detectors use light transmission through dental tissues to reveal carious lesions that are not visible under regular examination. Both technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and improve patient outcomes in preventive dentistry.</p></p>
