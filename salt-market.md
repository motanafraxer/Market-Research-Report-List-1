<p><p>The salt market encompasses various types, including sea salt, well mineral salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is harvested from evaporated seawater, rich in minerals and often used in culinary applications. Well mineral salt is extracted from underground mines, offering a diverse mineral profile and commonly used for both industrial and dietary purposes. Lake salt is derived from saline lakes, often featuring unique mineral compositions. Each type serves distinct needs in food production, health, and industry, reflecting diverse sources and applications.</p></p>
