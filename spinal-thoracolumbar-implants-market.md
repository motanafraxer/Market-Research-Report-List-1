<p><p>The Spinal Thoracolumbar Implants market comprises various types, each serving distinct surgical needs. Anterior Thoracolumbar implants are positioned from the front, providing support during spinal fusion. Posterior Thoracolumbar implants are accessed from the back, stabilizing the spine and facilitating alignment. Lumbar Interbody Solutions involve devices placed between vertebrae to enhance fusion and stability. Lumbar Instrumentation encompasses rods, screws, and plates that secure spinal structures, ensuring overall integrity and function during recovery from spinal conditions or injuries.</p></p>
