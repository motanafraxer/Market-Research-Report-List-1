<p><p>Radiation detection in industrial and scientific markets encompasses various technologies, namely Geiger counters, scintillation detectors, and solid-state detectors. Geiger counters are widely used for their simplicity and effectiveness in measuring ionizing radiation levels. Scintillation detectors utilize scintillating materials to convert radiation into light, offering high sensitivity and speed. Solid-state detectors, using semiconductor materials, provide excellent energy resolution and stability, making them suitable for precise measurements. Each type serves unique applications, enhancing safety and monitoring in diverse environments.</p></p>
