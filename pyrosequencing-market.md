<p><p>Pyrosequencing is a DNA sequencing technique that enables the determination of a nucleotide sequence using a process of light production. This method relies on the detection of released pyrophosphate during DNA synthesis, allowing real-time monitoring of nucleotides being incorporated into the growing DNA strand. It is known for its accuracy and ability to provide high-throughput results, making it valuable in various applications, including genomics, personalized medicine, and microbial analysis.</p><p>The Pyrosequencing Market is poised for robust growth, driven by advancements in sequencing technologies, increasing demand for personalized medicine, and the rising prevalence of genetic disorders. Enhanced focus on research and development within molecular diagnostics is also contributing to market expansion. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period. Additionally, trends such as the integration of bioinformatics tools with sequencing technologies and a growing emphasis on environmental genomics are shaping the future landscape of the industry. As costs decrease and accessibility improves, pyrosequencing is likely to see wider adoption across both clinical and research settings.</p></p>
