<p><p>The Guided Bone Regeneration (GBR) membrane market is categorized into two main types: absorbable membranes and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes are designed to degrade over time, eliminating the need for a second surgical procedure, which enhances patient comfort and convenience. In contrast, non-absorbable membranes provide longer-term support for bone regeneration but require a subsequent surgery for removal. Both types play crucial roles in dental and orthopedic procedures to promote effective bone healing and regrowth.</p></p>
