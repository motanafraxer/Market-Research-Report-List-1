<p><p>The Gel Imaging Instrument market includes two primary types: UV Gel Imaging Analysis Systems and Blue Light Gel Imaging Analysis Systems. UV systems utilize ultraviolet light to visualize and capture images of nucleic acid and protein gels, offering high sensitivity and resolution. Conversely, Blue Light systems use blue wavelengths for fluorescence detection, providing safer handling with reduced risk of DNA damage. Both types are instrumental in molecular biology, enabling researchers to analyze and document gel electrophoresis results accurately.</p></p>
