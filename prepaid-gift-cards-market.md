<p><p>The prepaid gift card market is divided into two main types: open loop and closed loop. Open loop cards, like those backed by major credit card networks, can be used at a wide range of retailers and locations, providing flexibility for consumers. In contrast, closed loop cards are restricted to specific brands or retailers, limiting use to their stores or platforms. This distinction influences consumer choice and the overall functionality of prepaid gift cards in the market.</p></p>
