<p><p>The salon-use hair dye products market encompasses three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide short-term color, lasting only until the next wash, making them ideal for experimentation. Semi-permanent dyes offer longer-lasting color, typically fading over several weeks without harsh chemicals. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft for long-lasting results, ensuring vibrant color that endures until new growth occurs. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and needs for hair coloring.</p></p>
