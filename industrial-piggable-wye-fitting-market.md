<p><p>The Industrial Piggable Wye Fitting market is segmented into two main types: Bypass Piggable Wye Fitting and Shutoff Piggable Wye Fitting. Bypass Piggable Wyes enable uninterrupted flow during maintenance by allowing inspection and cleaning tools, or pigs, to pass through while facilitating bypass flow. Conversely, Shutoff Piggable Wyes provide a shut-off mechanism for flow control during maintenance or emergencies, ensuring a secure closure while still accommodating the passage of pigs. Both types enhance pipeline efficiency and maintenance access.</p></p>
