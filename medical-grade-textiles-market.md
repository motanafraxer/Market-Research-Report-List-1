<p><strong>The Medical-Grade Textiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-woven Fabric</li><li>Woven Fabric</li><li>Knitted Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical-grade textiles market consists of three primary types: non-woven, woven, and knitted fabrics. Non-woven fabrics are created by bonding fibers through mechanical, chemical, or thermal means, making them ideal for disposable medical products like surgical gowns and masks. Woven fabrics are produced by interlacing yarns, offering durability and strength, suitable for applications such as drapes and patient apparel. Knitted fabrics are formed by inter-looping threads, providing flexibility and comfort, commonly used for surgical scrubs and compression garments.</p></p>
