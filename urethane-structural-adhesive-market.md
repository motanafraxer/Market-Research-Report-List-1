<p><strong>The Urethane Structural Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urethane structural adhesive market is segmented into two primary types: single-component and two-component adhesives. Single-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed before application, allowing for greater control over curing time and properties. Both types are valued for their high bonding strength and versatility across industries like automotive, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
