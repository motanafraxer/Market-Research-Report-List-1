<p><p>The Automatic Strapping Machines market is segmented into semi-automatic and fully automatic types. Semi-automatic strapping machines require manual intervention to feed packages, making them suitable for moderate volume operations. In contrast, fully automatic strapping machines integrate seamlessly into production lines, automatically feeding, strapping, and cutting without human intervention, catering to high-volume applications. Both types enhance packaging efficiency, but the choice depends on the level of automation needed and the production scale of the operation.</p></p>
