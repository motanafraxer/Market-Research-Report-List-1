<p><p>The polycarbonate compound market is primarily divided into clear and unclear polycarbonate types. Clear polycarbonate is known for its high transparency, impact resistance, and durability, making it ideal for applications like safety glasses, automotive components, and glazing. In contrast, unclear polycarbonate, often used in applications where transparency is less critical, provides robust performance in areas like electrical insulation and industrial parts. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, fulfilling specific performance needs.</p></p>
