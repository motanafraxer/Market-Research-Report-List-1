<p><strong>The Benchtop XRF Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersion</li><li>Energy Dispersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Benchtop XRF analyzers are categorized into two main types: wavelength dispersion (WDXRF) and energy dispersion (EDXRF). WDXRF utilizes a crystal to disperse emitted X-rays based on their wavelength, providing high resolution and precision, ideal for trace element analysis. In contrast, EDXRF employs a semiconductor detector to measure the energy of the emitted X-rays directly, offering faster results and versatility, suitable for a broader range of materials. Both technologies serve critical roles in material characterization and quality control across various industries.</p></p>
