<p><p>Automated windows can be categorized into three main types: mechanical, electric, and hybrid. Mechanical automated windows utilize springs or counterweights for operation, providing a simple and cost-effective solution. Electric automated windows rely on motorized systems, allowing for precise control and integration with smart home technologies. Hybrid automated windows combine both mechanical and electric systems, offering enhanced functionality and energy efficiency. Each type serves unique applications, catering to varying needs in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
